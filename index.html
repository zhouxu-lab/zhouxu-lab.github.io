Xu Zhou

Department of Food Science
Pennsylvania State University

I am an Assistant Professor at Penn State and lead the Intelligent Food Processing & AI Automation Lab. Our team integrates Food Science, Engineering, and Artificial Intelligence (AI) to advance next-generation food manufacturing.

Our group develops advanced food processing technologies and computational methods for next-generation food manufacturing systems. Our long-term goal is to enable autonomous, self-driving food engineering laboratories and factories. Currently, we focus on digital twins (sensing, simulation, human–AI collaboration, and robotics) for food processing and supply chains, with the aim of improving food quality, safety, and sustainability.

Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Scholar at UC Davis and the University of Washington. I earned my Ph.D. in Food Engineering and a bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering.

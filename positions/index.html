Open Positions

Master & Ph.D. Students

We are looking for motivated students to join our team. Ideal candidates should have a background or strong interest in:

  • Food Engineering
  • AI
  • Computer Simulation and Modeling
  • Food Safety and Quality

Please email me with the subject heading "Prospective Student - [Your Name]" and include your CV, transcript, and a 1-2 page cover letter with a description of your relevant past research experience, your future research interests, and why you want to work with me.

Postdoctoral Scholars

We are not currently conducting a formal postdoc search. However, if you believe you have a strong research fit and case for joining our lab, you can email me with the subject heading "Prospective PostDoc - [Your Name]" and include your CV and a 1-2 page cover letter with a description of your past research experience and what you would like to accomplish in our lab.
